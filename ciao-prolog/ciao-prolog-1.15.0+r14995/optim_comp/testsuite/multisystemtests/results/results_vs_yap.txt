           Performance comparison between Yap and optim-Ciao
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Machine: Intel Core Duo (my huygens laptop machine)

Conclusions: Ciao wins in some benchmarks, Yap in others. Yap wins in
the more interesting benchmarks (but Yap contains a lot of
optimizations that have not been included in Ciao) so I'd say that
Ciao may be a little slower than Yap but their performance are
comparable.

Of course, those results are limited to this benchmark set. With other
benchmarks that favor JIT-indexing and other Yap optimizations, Ciao
would probably not win.
